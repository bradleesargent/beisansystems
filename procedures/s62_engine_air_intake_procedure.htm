Engine air intake removal


Remove bank 1 & 2 MAF sensor electrical cable connectors.
Press in on connector side clips, pry connector at end, and pull off connector (flathead).


Disconnect banks 1 & 2 MAF electrical cables from clips under intake boot.


Loosen banks 1 & 2 intake duct hose clamps at intake manifold (6mm socket 1/4" / 1/4" ratchet & extension).


Pull bank 1 & 2 intake ducts from intake manifold.


Disconnect bank 1 & 2 intake air filter housing cover 4 clips each. Pry clips off (hand).


Lift up and remove bank 1 & 2 air filter box and intake ducts from engine compartment.
Remove air filters from air filter boxes.

Engine air intake installation


Clean bank 1 & 2 air filter boxes (towels & water based cleaner).


Install bank 1 & 2 air filters in air filter boxes.


Install bank 1 & 2 air filter box and intake ducts into engine compartment.


Mount banks 1 & 2 air filter box covers and fasten 4 mounting clips each.
Note: If cover not installing, remove cover and align and install air filter correctly.


Install bank 1 & 2 air duct ends onto intake manifold.
Align hose clamps to be accessible at each air duct side (picture).
Verify ducts are fully inserted onto intake manifold.


Position and tighten banks 1 & 2 air duct hose clamps (6mm socket 1/4" / 1/4" ratchet & extension).


Install bank 1 & 2 MAF electrical cables in clips under intake boots.


Install bank 1 & 2 MAF sensor electrical cable connectors.
Press connector onto MAF until it snaps into place.
